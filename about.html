About Our School

Our History

Founded in 1985, Phalaborwa Combined School has grown from a small community school to one of the most respected educational institutions in the region. What began as a primary school serving local families expanded in 1998 to include high school grades, creating a seamless educational journey for our students.

Over the years, we've maintained our commitment to academic excellence while adapting to the changing needs of our students and community. Our beautiful campus has evolved with modern facilities while preserving the warm, nurturing atmosphere that has always been our hallmark.

Our Facilities

Our campus features state-of-the-art learning environments designed to inspire and support students at every level:

  • Spacious, technology-equipped classrooms
  • Specialized science and computer labs
  • Comprehensive library with digital resources
  • Sports fields and gymnasium
  • Performing arts center
  • Secure playgrounds for younger students

We continuously invest in our facilities to ensure they meet the highest standards for 21st century learning.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, socially, and personally. We foster a love of learning, critical thinking skills, and ethical values that prepare students to be responsible citizens and leaders in their communities.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that sets the standard for excellence in combined schooling, where every student is known, valued, and inspired to excel. We envision graduates who are confident, compassionate, and equipped to meet the challenges of an interconnected world.
