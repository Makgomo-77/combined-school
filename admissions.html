Admissions

Join Our School Community

We're delighted you're considering Phalaborwa Combined School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Below you'll find all the information you need to begin your journey with us.

Admissions Process

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office or attend an open house to learn more about our programs and philosophy.

2

Application

Submit the completed application form along with required documents and the application fee.

3

Assessment

Students may be assessed to determine appropriate grade placement (Grade 1 and above).

4

Acceptance

Receive notification of acceptance and complete enrollment paperwork.
